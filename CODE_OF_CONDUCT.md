# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in OPENCLAW Project welcoming, respectful, and
productive for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or technology
preferences.

## Our standards

Examples of encouraged behavior:

- Treating others with respect and empathy.
- Giving constructive, actionable feedback.
- Assuming good intent while being accountable for impact.
- Welcoming new contributors and explaining project context.
- Keeping discussions focused on technical merit and user safety.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Insults, personal attacks, or deliberately inflammatory comments.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or maintainer work.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may remove comments,
reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this Code
of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers
through the project's preferred private channel. If no private channel exists,
open an issue asking for a maintainer contact without including sensitive
personal details.

Reports will be handled with discretion. Maintainers should review reports
promptly and take proportionate action.
